Our History

JC Barber and Beauty Supply started with a Dream, vision, passion of one person and a group of amazing people who have become family and has helped us become who we are right now.

Our CEO, Julio Santos went from selling blades, brushes and other essentials to barbershops and salons in a 2-door car (Mustang) during his lunch break in 2006, to and amazing business owner with 2 Stores, a Warehouse and 4 Mobile Stores, these visit salons and barbershops in all Central Florida.

Our mission is to support and provide all professionals and business partners all over USA and Internationally, with the best products and brands in the market, carrying 100+ brands between the store and warehouse and adding many more in the future.
